You'll download a single file. Double-click it and it opens right in your web browser — nothing to install and no account to make. Everything works the same offline.

Where is my plan saved?

Your plan is stored privately in your own browser on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use the built-in PDF export to keep a permanent copy or move it between devices.

Will it work on my phone or tablet?

Yes. The layout adapts to any screen, so you can plan on a laptop and review on your phone.

No. It's an educational planning tool that organizes widely available information and links you to the official bodies. Requirements vary by state and program, so always confirm the specifics with your state board of nursing and each school.
